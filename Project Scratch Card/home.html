PROJECT SCRATCH CARD

Beginning on the 1st December 2023 I am delighted to say I am going to take on the challenge of buying a scratch card every day for a whole year. I have decided to record this process on this website, updating it every Thursday.
I will provide a photograph of each scratch card purchased, then, at the end of the week, produce a video of the results. This video will also be uploaded to this site.

The motivation to undertake this project forms from the desire that I want to purchase something daily. The need for a newspaper to start the day is old fashioned with news being available 24/7.
My aim is to go to a shop each day at the same time, bringing much needed routine to my life and, with it, the idea of winning big.

There are three rules which must be followed

  1. Only one scratch card can be brought each day*
  2. £15 must be spent every week, no more, no less**
  3. No other gambling is to be undertaken throughout the year***




TC's *Catching up on Bank Holidays / shop not open or personal holidays / no shop nearby is permitted
         ** This is in the form of 1* £5 4* £2 and 2* £1
         ***Twice weekly Euromillions ticket is acceptable as I have set numbers
